How to adjust your display:

  1. Look at the test pattern above
  2. Use your monitor's physical buttons to enter its settings menu
  3. Adjust brightness until you can barely see the circle in the darkest area
  4. Adjust contrast until you can clearly distinguish between all gradient boxes
  5. Save your monitor settings when done
You should be able to see all boxes from 1-10 while box A should be barely visible
